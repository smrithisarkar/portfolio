Project Overview

Developed an end-to-end interactive dashboard solution to monitor real-time credit card operations and customer demographics. This project focuses on identifying revenue drivers and risk factors through deep-dive transaction analysis[cite: 15].

Key Transaction Insights

  • Revenue by Card: The "Blue" card category is the primary revenue driver, contributing $47M to the total revenue.
  • Quarterly Trends: Identified consistent revenue performance across the year, with Q4 peaking at $14.5M.
  • Expenditure Types: "Bills," "Entertainment," and "Fuel" were identified as the top three categories for customer spending.
  • Transaction Modes: "Swipe" is the most preferred method, accounting for $36M in transaction volume.

Customer Segmentation Analysis

  • Demographics: Male customers contribute $31M in revenue compared to $26M from female customers.
  • Age & Income: The 40-50 age group and "High" income groups show the highest engagement and revenue contribution.
  • Professional Profiles: Businessmen and White-collar workers are the top-earning segments for the bank.
  • Education Influence: Graduates contribute the highest revenue ($23M) among all education levels.

Technical Expertise Applied

  • DAX: Created measures for weekly revenue tracking, quarterly trends, and interest analysis[cite: 11, 15].
  • Data Modeling: Optimized relationships between transaction and customer tables for high-performance reporting[cite: 11, 18].
  • Visualization: Designed dynamic slicers for Gender, Income Level, and Card Type to enable granular business insights[cite: 11, 17].
