Project Overview

This exploratory data analysis (EDA) project identifies the purchasing patterns of customers during the festive season. Using Python's data science stack, I cleaned a dataset of 11,000+ records to pinpoint exactly who the "Ideal Customer" is for targeted marketing.

Key Insights from EDA

  • Gender Dominance: Females account for the highest number of orders and spending capacity.
  • Age Bracket: Most buyers belong to the 26-35 years age group, representing the most profitable demographic.
  • Regional Hubs: UP, Maharashtra, and Karnataka contribute to over 50% of total revenue.
  • Industry Influence: Customers working in IT, Healthcare, and Aviation are the top spenders.
  • Winning Categories: Food, Clothing, and Electronics are the top three product categories.

Technical Expertise Applied

  • Data Cleaning: Used Pandas to handle null values and drop unnecessary columns (Status, Unnamed).
  • Statistical Visualization: Implemented Seaborn for complex hue-based bar charts and countplots.
  • Business Intelligence: Converted raw CSV data into a clear "Target Persona" profile for retail stakeholders.
