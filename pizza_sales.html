Project Overview

This project focuses on identifying sales trends and operational peaks for a pizza retail business. Using SQL for data extraction and Excel for advanced visualization, I transformed raw transaction [cite_start]data into actionable insights for inventory management and marketing strategies.

Operational Insights

  • Busiest Days: Order volumes peak significantly on Fridays and Saturdays.
  • Peak Hours: Maximum orders are received between 12:00 PM – 1:00 PM and 5:00 PM – 6:00 PM.
  • Category Performance: The "Classic" category contributes the highest percentage of sales (26.91%).
  • Size Preferences: Large-sized pizzas are the most preferred by customers, accounting for 45.89% of total sales.

Product Performance Analysis

  • Best Seller: "The Classic Deluxe Pizza" is the top-selling product by quantity (2,453 pizzas).
  • Least Seller: "The Brie Carre Pizza" was identified as the lowest-selling item, suggesting a need for menu review.
  • Revenue Mix: Balanced sales distribution across Chicken (23.96%), Supreme (25.46%), and Veggie (23.68%) categories.
